The Growing Obesity Problem

In 1990, obesity emerged as a serious issue as the BMI of the average person skyrocketed. Today, obesity is still a huge problem. Over 2 out of every 3 American adults are overweight or obese. Additionally, America spends about $150 billion dollars on obesity-related issues.

  • Obese people are 10X more likely to get Heart diease
  • Obesity is major cause of type 2 diabetes
  • Obesity causes high blood pressure
  • Obesity could cause sleep apnea which interrupts sleep at night and makes you sleepy during day
